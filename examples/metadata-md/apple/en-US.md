## Description

Focus Garden is a simple focus timer that helps students, makers, and remote workers stay consistent without making productivity feel heavy.

Start a session, choose a task, and grow your garden while you stay focused. The longer you protect your attention, the more your garden fills with plants, trees, and small rewards.

Focus Garden helps you:

- Plan short focus sessions before you start
- Use Pomodoro-style timers for study, work, and deep focus
- Track daily progress with simple streaks
- Review completed sessions by task or project
- Build a calmer routine around focused work

Whether you are studying for exams, writing code, reading, or trying to spend less time switching apps, Focus Garden gives you a lightweight way to begin and finish focused sessions.

Start a timer. Grow something useful.
